Welcome to Loanzen

Loanzen is an RBI registered NBFC operating in the field of financing used commercial vehicles. The company is focused on bringing to market customised credit products that address the needs of small businesses in the logistics sector and businesses operating vehicles for captive use. Loanzen aims to make the process easy for the customer with rapid loan decisioning aided by automated loan workflow, transparent communication, tracking of the process and customer support during the process. The company is currently operating in Bengaluru, Karnataka.
